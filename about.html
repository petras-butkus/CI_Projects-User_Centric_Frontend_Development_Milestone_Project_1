About the Irish Angler's Guide

Our Mission

At the Irish Angler's Guide, our mission is to be the definitive online resource for anyone interested in fishing in Ireland. We aim to provide comprehensive, accurate, and inspiring information to both seasoned anglers and newcomers, helping them discover the unparalleled beauty and abundance of Ireland's fishing waters.

We are dedicated to promoting sustainable and responsible angling practices, ensuring the preservation of Ireland's rich aquatic ecosystems for future generations. Our goal is to enhance your fishing experience by providing all the knowledge you need for a successful and enjoyable trip.

What We Offer

  • Detailed Fishing Guides: Information on various fishing types, including Salmon, Trout, Sea Angling, Pike, and Coarse Fishing.
  • Best Fishing Locations: Insights into prime fishing spots across Ireland's rivers, lakes, and coastlines.
  • Gear & Technique Advice: Recommendations for equipment and effective fishing techniques for different species.
  • Regulations: Up-to-date information on necessary licenses, permits, and conservation rules.
  • Inspiring Content: Articles and tips to inspire your next angling adventure in Ireland.
